ABOUT US

Company Description:

EasyShift is a technology company specializing in part-time workforce management solutions. The company’s goal is to create a user-friendly, online-based platform designed to streamline scheduling, log hours, and communication for businesses with part-time employees. By simplifying the administrative aspects of managing part-timers, it helps companies lower the chance of conflict and improve employee satisfaction. With the right tools for managers and employees, our system will provide better access to their schedules and shift information, making it seamless for part-timers to do their assigned tasks.

Mission:

EasyShift’s mission is to provide an efficient, flexible, and user-friendly platform that simplifies shift management. We aim to improve the work environment where every shift is well organized and communication is clear.

Vision:

EasyShift imagines a future where businesses of all sizes can seamlessly manage their part-timer workforce with ease. Our goal is to eventually be the best solution for managing part-timers.
